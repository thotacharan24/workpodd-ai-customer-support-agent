# Refund Policy

1. Refunds allowed within 30 days of purchase.
2. Clearance items cannot be refunded.
3. Digital products cannot be refunded.
4. Subscription payments cannot be refunded.
5. More than 3 refunds in last 90 days causes denial.
6. Fraud flagged customers are denied.
7. Defective products qualify even after 30 days.
8. Missing order information causes denial.
9. Pending shipments cannot be refunded.
10. Agent must provide rule citation when making decisions.
